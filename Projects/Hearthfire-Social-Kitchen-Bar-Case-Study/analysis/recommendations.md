# Recommendations — Hearthfire Social Kitchen + Bar

This document outlines targeted, high-ROI recommendations based on observed data patterns.

---

## 1. Bar Program Optimization

### Actions
- Pair **experienced** bartenders with new hires during peak services.
- Build HH and Dinner into a **two-round strategy** (planned upsells + pacing).
- Introduce 2–3 “Hero Cocktails” with fast build times and high visual appeal.

### Expected Impact
- Stronger bar attachment rate → +2–3% weekly margin  
- Faster pacing → +$400–$700 additional bar revenue weekly

---

## 2. Menu Engineering

### Actions
- Reposition desserts and N/A beverages as **margin partners** to entrées.
- Reduce prep volume and portion variance on halibut and gnocchi.
- Introduce one **medium-cost, high-margin entrée** to balance the category mix.

### Expected Impact
- Entrée margin uplift of 3–5 percentage points  
- 10–15% increase in dessert + N/A beverage pairing

---

## 3. Waste & Prep Control

### Actions
- Stagger BOH shifts on Saturday:
  - Half prep in AM, half prep pre-dinner.
- Implement **Daily Usage Forecasts** instead of relying solely on pars.
- Make halibut a “sell out” special with a cap.

### Expected Impact
- Reduce weekly waste by 25–35%  
- Saves ~$120–$180 per week  
- Cuts into Sunday waste trend

---

## 4. Labor Alignment

### Actions
- Reduce Sunday late-night staffing; consider eliminating Sunday LN entirely.
- Stagger prep cooks on Sat + Sun based on real demand curves.
- Increase FOH cross-training for drink running + expediting.

### Expected Impact
- Labor % drops from 41.9% → target 32–34% on Sundays  
- Consistent weeklong labor % in the 28–32% range

---

## 5. Daypart Optimization

### Actions
- Create a **Tuesday Dinner Feature** that pairs with a high-margin bar item.
- Launch **Thursday Late Night Industry Night**.
- Build Sunday around brunch + early dinner, with LN closed.

### Expected Impact
- Tuesday dinner becomes revenue-neutral instead of margin-negative.
- Thursday becomes another “bar anchor” like Friday.
- Sunday stabilizes without high labor drag.

---

## 6. Monitoring & Continuous Improvement

### Actions
- Weekly margin dashboard (bar vs. food vs. waste vs. labor).
- Track:
  - Rounds per guest  
  - Waste per category  
  - Prep accuracy  
  - Promo-driven mix shifts

### Expected Impact
- Early detection of waste spikes  
- Better staff scheduling  
- Better forecasting accuracy
